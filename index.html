Moderation Guidelines (What to expect from us)

  • Temporary Bans - of approximately 10 minutes may be applied without warning in order to quickly shut down fights and other drama.
    • Temporary bans are not a form of punishment in and of themselves.
    • They neither require, nor count towards warnings for extended bans. However, you may recieve a formal warning at the same time if you are misbehaving.
    • Yes, you might be recieve a temporary ban mistakenly, if so, PM the moderator, and they'll sort it out ASAP, please be patient.
    • Any moderator has discretion to shorten or lift a temporary ban at any time. (Within reason)
  • Each user will recieve at least 3 formal warnings before receiving an extended ban (does not include 10 minute bans).
  • In general, extended bans will applied for the following durations, in order: First ban is 1, second ban is 2-3 days, third ban is permanent.
    However, these are just guides, and specific penalties are at the discretion of the acting moderator(s).
  • Complaints or problems with moderator decisions or behaviour will ONLY be handled in PMs. Be it with that mod, or another. Discussing these matters in the room will NOT be tolerated.
  • We are here to maintain a pleasant and welcoming mood and atmosphere in the room, not just to enforce the rules. We may take action against acts that aren't yet covered by the rules. Within reason.
